Dior: A Legacy of Elegance

Christian Dior founded the House of Dior in 1946, revolutionizing fashion with the "New Look" that emphasized femininity. Beyond clothing, Dior expanded into perfumes, accessories, and cosmetics, with iconic products like Miss Dior. Over the decades, designers like Yves Saint Laurent and Maria Grazia Chiuri have upheld Dior's blend of tradition and modernity. Today, Dior remains a global symbol of luxury, creativity, and Parisian chic, embodying excellence and artistry.
